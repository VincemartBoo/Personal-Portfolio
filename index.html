Project "Banowa"

"Project Banowa" is a sleek and intuitive weather application designed to provide users with accurate and up-to-date weather information at their fingertips. Named after the Filipino word for weather, "Banowa" embodies its essence, offering a seamless experience tailored to meet the diverse weather needs of its users. With a clean interface and easy navigation, users can effortlessly access current weather conditions and extended outlooks for any location worldwide.

Project "Lacsap"

"Project Lacsap" is a user-friendly calculator WebApp designed to make mathematical computations effortless and intuitive. The name "Lacsap" pays tribute to the pioneering work of Blaise Pascal, with a clever reversal of his name, symbolizing innovation and inspiration. With its clean interface and responsive design, Project Lacsap caters to users of all levels, whether they're students tackling homework assignments or professionals needing quick calculations on the go.
